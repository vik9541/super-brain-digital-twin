## âœ… Email Best Practices

### General Guidelines
1. **Keep it short**: 200-300 words max
2. **Clear subject line**: Specific and compelling
3. **Personalize**: Reference their work, show you've done research
4. **Clear ask**: What do you want from them?
5. **Make it easy**: Links, attachments, calendar links
6. **Follow up**: If no response in 7 days, polite follow-up

### Timing
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times**: 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
- **Avoid**: Mondays (email backlog), Fridays (weekend mode)

- âœ… **Specific**: "Seeking Technical Feedback on AI Contact Manager"
